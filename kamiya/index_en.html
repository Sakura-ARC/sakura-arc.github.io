STORY

Story Overview

July 2025, Summer in Fukuoka. A listless Sunday evening.
A single phone call heralds the arrival of a new case.

Saki Namihana, a high school girl, has gone missing.
While the police treat it as a voluntary runaway, a disturbing video leaked onto the internet transforms their peaceful daily life into a torrent of information.

The weight of a life, consumed and drowned out by morbid curiosity.
―― What did we see, and what did we not see.

THE AGENCY

About the Agency

Just an 8-minute walk from Hakata Station, an apartment in a building over 40 years old—Genest Hakata-Higashi, Room 505.
That is the "Kamiya Detective Agency," run by you—Kengo Kamiya, a former detective—and your assistant, Hikaru Omuta.

A few months after opening, the first request arrived at your quiet office.
It was the mysterious disappearance of a high school girl.

A realistic world is expressed through photography based on real-life scenery in Fukuoka.
You must spot the contradictions hidden behind testimonies and rumors, layering your reasoning from fragmented information.

The one to uncover the truth is you, standing in the shoes of Detective Kamiya.

CHARACTERS

Main Characters

Detective / Boss

Kengo Kamiya

The head of the Kamiya Detective Agency. A 29-year-old former police detective who excels in calm, logical deduction. Although he opened the agency to fulfill a childhood dream, he currently has zero clients. He is notably handsome.

Assistant / PR Manager

Hikaru Omuta

A 19-year-old assistant. Standing at a petite 140cm, she is frequently mistaken for a child. A gamer with a slightly otaku personality. She expresses her affection for Kamiya openly. She is currently updating X to promote the agency.

Missing Girl / Gyaru

Saki Namihana

A 17-year-old high school girl who went missing on the day of the closing ceremony before summer break. She adopted a "Gyaru" style a year ago, drastically altering her social circle. What on earth happened to her?

Client / Mother

Mariko Namihana

Saki's mother. A conservative woman who is easily worried. Despite the rift that grew between her and her daughter, she loves her deeply. She hires Kamiya to investigate the disappearance of her daughter.
